# The G.U.A.R.D.S. Method
> to creating settlements
## **G — Governance**
Who’s in charge, and how?
- Is it a monarchy, council, elected mayor, a merchant guild, a theocracy?
- Is leadership just or corrupt? Local or influenced by outside powers?
- Example questions:
    - Who enforces the law?
    - Are there taxes or tithes?
    - Is there conflict between leaders or factions?
## **U — Utilities & Infrastructure**
What keeps the town running day-to-day?
- Water, food, roads, bridges, sanitation, mills, etc.
- Power sources: waterwheels, magic stones, manual labor?
- Includes defense structures like walls, towers, watchposts.
## **A — Artisans & Economy**
What do people make, sell, or trade?
- Blacksmiths, cobblers, miners, brewers, etc.
- What is the town’s main export or claim to fame?
- What’s rare, expensive, or in high demand?
## **R — Residents**
Who lives here? What's life like?
- Demographics: race, age spread, class division.
- Community values, local sayings, festivals, superstitions.
- What's daily life like for the common folk?
## **D — Danger & Drama**
What’s the tension, mystery, or threat?
- Bandits? Monsters? A corrupt guild? A forgotten curse?
- Could be subtle (economic collapse) or overt (beast attacks).
- Adventure hooks, secrets, ancient ruins, rumors.
## **S — Surroundings**
What’s nearby that shapes the town?
- Geography: rivers, mountains, forests, ruins, borders.
- Trade routes, nearby cities, rival settlements.
- Environmental threats: floods, rockslides, magical fallout.

---

What do we need to know?
- Species
- cultures
- anyone important born here
- who lives there
- are there any items of note in this settlement
- what organizations are here
And we can add details, like
- Geology: geological features, climate, natural resources
- Ecology: biomes, animal migrations, habitability
- Land use: how is the land here used? Agriculture, mining, commercial, gardens, ..
- Economy: GDP, economic model, currency, imports/exports
- Demographic: which people occupy or visit this location
- Energy: Are there any sources of power, like electricity, magic, consumable power, and how much
- Security: How is this location defended, if at all?
